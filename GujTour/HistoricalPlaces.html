Historical Places

Terms like history, heritages and monuments are synonymous to Gujarat. The glorious Gujarat is home to many ancient city ruins, palaces, forts and tomb, which stands proudly bearing testimony to the golden era of dynasties. Since its inception, landscapes of Gujarat are ruled by rulers of many dynasties, invaders and sellers.

Gujarat's past is a part of its current terrains, evidenced by the ancient and historical ruins scattered across the country. In Gujarat, one can see the massive forts, charmings havelis, stunning stepwells and many other historical sites that showcase some of the relics of the land.
