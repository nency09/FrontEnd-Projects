Impact (2021-2022)

CARE has been working in India for 70 years, focusing on alleviating poverty and social exclusion. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programmes in health, education, livelihoods, and disaster preparedness and response. We are part of the CARE International Confederation, working in over 100 countries for a world where all people live with dignity and security.
